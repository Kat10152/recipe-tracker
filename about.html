About Recipe Tracker

A simple, personal space to save, browse, and create the recipes you love.

🥗

What is Recipe Tracker?

Recipe Tracker is a lightweight tool for home cooks and food lovers. Browse a growing collection of recipes, save your favourites, and add your own creations — all stored right in your browser with no sign-up required.

🔖

Save What You Love

Found a recipe you want to come back to? Hit the bookmark icon and it's saved instantly. Your saved recipes stay in your browser so they're always there when you need them.

✏️

Add Your Own Recipes

Have a family recipe or a personal creation you want to keep handy? Use the "Create Your Own Recipe" button on the recipes page to add it to your collection.

🔍

Search & Filter

Looking for something specific? Search by recipe name or cuisine, and filter by difficulty or cuisine type to quickly find exactly what you're in the mood to cook.
